FAQ's

How does the auto click work?▼

You simply toggle the switch under the mouse, press the left click button, and it begins clicking repeatedly until you release the button or toggle it off.

Is it compatible with Mac?▼

Yes, the NEXUS X1 is plug-and-play compatible with macOS, Windows, and Linux. No drivers needed.

Can I adjust the DPI?▼

Yes, use the DPI button located behind the scroll wheel to cycle through 800, 1200, 1600, and 2400 DPI.
